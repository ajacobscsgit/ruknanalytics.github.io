E-commerce

Dashboard & Automation Overhaul

A high-growth online retailer was drowning in fragmented reporting, manual campaign tracking, and slow executive decision cycles.

Overview

The client sold through multiple channels and relied on spreadsheets for inventory, sales, and marketing performance. Data was scattered, delayed, and difficult to trust.

Problem

  • Manual reports updated weekly instead of in real time
  • No consolidated view of revenue, margins, and marketing ROI
  • Multiple spreadsheets caused version conflicts and lost data

Solution

  • Cleaned and standardized sales, inventory, and marketing data
  • Built a single executive dashboard for revenue, conversion, and retention
  • Automated data refreshes and created scheduled reporting workflows
  • Implemented KPI tracking for weekly decision reviews

Tools Used

Google Sheets Excel Python Looker Studio

Results

  • Reduced manual reporting labor by 80%
  • Delivered real-time revenue visibility across channels
  • Improved executive decision speed by 35%
  • Created a repeatable process for ongoing growth monitoring

Before vs After

Before

Multiple spreadsheets, delayed insights, manual reconciliation, and unclear revenue performance.

After

Automated reporting, centralized KPIs, trustable dashboards, and faster leadership alignment.
